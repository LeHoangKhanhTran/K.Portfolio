Air Max Day is on March 26, the day Nike celebrates its Air Max cushioning technology by launching new sneaker models, including commemorating the release of the first Air Max 1 in 1987. Nike began celebrating this occasion in 2014.

To celebrate that cultural tradition, on March 26, 2017, the Vietnam Airmax Community and RetroKid organized Airmax Day with the theme “Love is in the air” to spread the passion for sneakers in general and the Nike Air Max line in particular. The event featured many exciting activities such as an ID Contest (designing your own pair of Nike Air Max according to your preferences), raffles, and more.

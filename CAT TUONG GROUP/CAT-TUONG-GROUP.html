Cat Tuong Group’s New year gift and calendar set reimagines the company’s proud journey of growth and its cherished values through an artful fusion of traditional calligraphy and the Four Noble Plants—Pine, Chrysanthemum, Bamboo, and Apricot Blossom—iconic symbols of luck, longevity, and prosperity in East Asian culture.

Each calligraphic stroke is rendered with finesse, honoring timeless artistic heritage while conveying sincere New Year wishes and reflecting Cát Tường Group’s spirit of resilience and advancement. The presence of the Four Noble Plants further enriches the collection, bringing the elegance of nature and traditional painting to life, and spreading a profound message of harmony and prosperity.

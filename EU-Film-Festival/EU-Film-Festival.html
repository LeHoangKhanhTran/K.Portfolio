EUFF 2023 features nearly 20 contemporary European films, including several award-winning titles. Inspired by the theme of “journeys”, the festival tells stories of parents and children, athletes, artists, writers, emigrants, friends, and strangers as they travel across Europe to discover their identity, community, and heritage — and enjoy unforgettable experiences along the way. Through their stories, Europe is shown in all its colorful and diverse beauty.

EUFF 2023 is organized by the Delegation of the European Union to Vietnam, in cooperation with embassies and cultural institutions representing EU Member States, and the Embassy of Ukraine in Vietnam, with support from the EU Policy and Outreach Partnership initiative (EUPOP).

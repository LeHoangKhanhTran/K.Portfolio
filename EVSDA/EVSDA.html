Initiated by the Delegation of the European Union to Vietnam, the EVSDA project seeks to foster a vibrant sustainable design community in Vietnam, supporting the country’s inclusive and sustainable growth—one of the core priorities in EU–Vietnam cooperation.

The award creates a unique platform uniting four design disciplines—Sustainable Architecture, Product Design, Communication Design, and Breakthrough Initiatives—opening space for cross-disciplinary exchange, fresh inspiration, and accessible yet foundational conversations about the true essence of design.

Inspired by the traditional fan dances of both Vietnam and Korea, Lotte Hotel Hanoi introduces a vibrant cultural performance that blends the elegance and colors of two rich heritages.

The 2025 Mooncake Collection celebrates timeless traditions and the warm bonds between people—extending beyond family and national borders. Through the iconic “national fan dance” the collection symbolizes friendship and unity between Vietnam and Korea, carrying forward values that connect hearts across cultures.

With the message “Digital Enterprise, Leading Financial Group,” MBBank aims to once again affirm its position within the financial sector. The visual concept is inspired by the bank’s own logo—the star. This star is formed from pixels, symbolizing the digitalization of the enterprise, while its forward motion represents MBBank’s continuous effort to advance, innovate, and perfect itself.

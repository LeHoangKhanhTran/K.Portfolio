The courage to push beyond one’s own limits is, in itself, more precious than any medal. The 2024 Lunar New Year calendar recreates and honors 12 defining moments in modern Japanese sports—moments that embody the unyielding Samurai spirit, steadfast and never retreating. With this, we extend our heartfelt wishes to our valued customers and partners for a new year of resilience and strength—turning challenges into opportunities, making spectacular comebacks, and achieving countless successes.

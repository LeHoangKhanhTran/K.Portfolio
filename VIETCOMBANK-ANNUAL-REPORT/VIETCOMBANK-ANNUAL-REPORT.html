Inspired by nature and the imagery of turning pages, the design symbolizes Vietcombank’s journey of sustainable development—one that grows in harmony with nature and the environment, from which its proud achievements are written. The nature-inspired visuals, expressed through earthy brown tones and soft, organic transitions, evoke a sense of warmth and familiarity. At the same time, the turning-page motif highlights Vietcombank’s ongoing story—its journeys and successes that will continue to unfold in the chapters ahead.

Inspired by that spirit, the 2026 Lunar New Year calendar presented by Vietcombank brings to life the image of the Quý Mã zodiac through rich slices of Vietnamese life. This collection is the heartfelt collaboration between young creative artists and seasoned historians–cultural researchers, representing two generations joining hands to preserve national heritage in a renewed form. Each artwork harmonizes traditional folk inspiration with contemporary expression, portraying the Quý Mã as a guardian figure that spreads blessings and accompanies the Vietnamese people as they ride the winds, move forward, and welcome new victories in an era of national ascent.
